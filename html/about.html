History

Decades ago Silkar started with small scale project such as fitting streetlights and power distribution networks. Bussiness grew as customers began to trust us with larger and larger scale projects, until today we are regarded as one of PLN's (The State Electricity Company) most reliable partners, able to handle projects of any size, including the construction of power stations, open and cable transmission networks ranging from 1 KV to 150 KV and diesel and photo voltaic generators.


We enjoy the same reputation for quality in the mechanical engineering sector, where our range of services includes water and gas pipe laying as well as air conditioner system and elevator installation.


The reason for our dramatic progress is simple. We regard every project given to us as equally important. Employees of Silkar are noted for their dedication and hard work, and are therefore willing to go that little bit further to make sure that the client are well and truly satisfied.


After more than decades experienced, Silkar have become a highly respected name in the field of civil engineering. Time after time, satisfied clients come back to us with their follow up order,

safe in the knowledge that any project given to PT SILKAR NATIONAL will proceed smoothly and be completed with the highest degree of responsibility. Having completed a wide range of complex projects we feel comfident in our ability to solve any difficulties that may occur during construction.

Such projects range from main road and bridge building to irrigation work and runway, construction all of which require the expertise of a highly committed and dependable organization. Our workforce are not only highly experienced, but also take a real pride in their work. Not suprisingly, they look back with satisfaction on their excellent track record.

Our outstanding success in this field derives from our close cooperation with international telecommunications companies, allowing the transfer of state of the art trechnology. Our telecommunications achievements have been rewarded in a number of ways. Silkar are especially proud of their position as market leader in the use of optical fibre cable to complete various terrestrial projects. The company have also received recognition from abroad, having been a ppointed sole agent for VARIAN'S wide range of satellite transmission equipment.
